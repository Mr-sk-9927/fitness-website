healthy life

Regular workouts build strength and endurance.Aim for at least 3–5 sessions per week. Pair workouts with nutritious meals. Focus on lean proteins, whole grains, and fresh vegetables.

workout

Beginner: Full-body workouts 2–3 times a week. Intermediate: Split routines (e.g., upper/lower body splits). Advanced: Targeted muscle group training with progressive overload. Muscle Gain: Heavy lifting with lower reps and high protein intake.
